Project Info

Welcome to Cryptodash! This project was born out of Jon's ETL project and is really being built so he can track his cryptomillions. Our main sources of data were the following: https://coinmarketcap.com and https://www.bitmex.com/

Some Definitions

Shorts Minus Longs

a high number is bullish

Bitfinex Shorts

Bitfinex is a margin exchange which discloses the number of short positions outstanding. The higher the number of outstanding short positions the higher the probability is that a short squeeze will be triggered, therefore higher number of shorts = bullish.

Bitfinex Leveraged Longs

Bitfinex is a margin exchange which discloses their outstanding leveraged long positions. When traders employ leverage this reflects bullish sentiment but margin positions tends to be shorter term in nature with tighter stop losses, therefore higher amount of margin longs = bearish.

Bitmex Funding Rate

Funding rate for the popular Bitmex XBT perpetual swap contract.a perpetual The way they keep the price of the perpetual swap in-line with underlying BTC is the funding rate. Longs pay the shorts a positive funding rate 3x/day or vice versa if the rate is negative. A high rate means folks are more bullish and incentivizes folks to take the short side of the contract and vice-versa.

Bitcoin Dominance

this metric reflects the total Bitcoin market cap/total crypto market cap. It tends to be somewhat range bound and mean reverting so when the ratio gets to the high-end of the range, prepare for alts season and vice-versa.
